Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Wealthr ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Wealthr app available at getwealthr.com and on the Google Play Store.

1. Data We Collect

Wealthr is designed with privacy at its core. All financial data you enter — including bank balances, portfolio holdings, crypto, budget categories, bills, pensions and property — is stored only on your device using your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB. We do not collect, transmit or store any of your personal financial data on our servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address or personal details
  • We do not collect your financial data or account information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not sell data to third parties
  • We do not have access to any data you enter into the app

3. Wealthr Pro Subscription

Wealthr Pro is a paid subscription available at £1.49/month. Payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not handle or store your payment details directly. Google's privacy policy applies to all billing transactions. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time via Google Play.

4. Third Party Services

Wealthr uses the following third party services to provide live data. These services are only contacted when you actively use the relevant feature:

  • Twelve Data (twelvedata.com) — stock price data
  • CoinGecko (coingecko.com) — cryptocurrency price data
  • ExchangeRate-API (open.er-api.com) — currency exchange rates
  • Yahoo Finance, BBC, Sky News, CNBC, Bloomberg, Guardian, Reuters, FT, Investing.com — news RSS feeds
  • Bank of England (bankofengland.co.uk) — base rate data
  • Land Registry (landregistry.data.gov.uk) — UK sold property prices
  • Blockstream (blockstream.info) — Bitcoin wallet lookups (only if you use that feature)
  • AllOrigins / corsproxy.io — CORS proxy services used to fetch data from the above sources

Each service's own privacy policy applies to requests made to their APIs. We do not send personal identifiers (name, email, account details) to any of these services. However, when you use features that query a third-party API — for example refreshing stock prices, looking up a Bitcoin wallet balance, or fetching news — the request URL (including the ticker symbol or wallet address) is visible to the relevant service, and to the CORS proxy (AllOrigins or corsproxy.io) when one is used to route the request. If you prefer to avoid this, do not use the Portfolio, Crypto, Wallet Lookup or News features.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable bill reminders, the app will request permission to send push notifications. Notifications are generated locally on your device by the service worker and do not pass through our servers. You can revoke notification permission at any time in your device settings.

6. API Keys

If you enter a Twelve Data API key in Settings, this key is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers. It is sent directly to Twelve Data's API when you refresh stock prices.

7. Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored in your device's localStorage and IndexedDB. This means your data stays on your device and is never backed up to the cloud by us. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, your data will be permanently deleted. We strongly recommend using the Export Backup feature in Settings regularly.

8. Children's Privacy

Wealthr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at wealthr42@outlook.com

Wealthr is a personal finance tracking tool only. It does not provide financial advice. Always consult a qualified financial adviser before making investment or financial decisions.
