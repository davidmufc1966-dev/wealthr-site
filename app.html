❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Tap a question to expand the answer.
🔒 Is my data safe?
›
Yes — all your financial data is stored only on your device using localStorage and IndexedDB. Nothing is ever sent to our servers or shared with anyone. We do not collect, store or transmit your financial data.
📡 Does the app work offline?
›
Most features work offline. Stock prices, crypto prices, market data and news need an internet connection to refresh, but all your saved data is always accessible without one.
👀 What is Demo mode?
›
Demo mode loads a realistic set of sample data so you can explore all features before entering your own. A blue "Demo Mode" banner appears at the bottom. Tap "Start with my own data →" to clear the demo data and start fresh. Adding items is disabled while in demo mode.
🔐 How do I set up PIN protection?
›
Go to ⚙️ Settings → Security → Set PIN. Enter and confirm a 4-digit PIN. The app will lock automatically and require the PIN to open. You can also enable fingerprint unlock if your device supports it. To remove the PIN go to Security → Remove PIN.
⭐Pro & Subscription
What is Wealthr Pro?
›
Wealthr Pro is a £2.99/month subscription that removes all free-plan limits and unlocks CSV import/export, price alerts, recurring transactions and more.

Free plan includes: 2 bank accounts · 3 portfolio positions · 3 crypto holdings · 3 bills · 3 subscriptions · 2 income sources · 5 budget categories · 1 pension · 1 property. All other features (news, market data, charts, PDF export, JSON backup, savings goals, loans, credit cards) are unlimited on the free plan.

Pro unlocks: unlimited everything, plus CSV import/export and price alerts. Payment handled securely by Stripe — cancel anytime.
How do I upgrade to Pro?
›
Tap the ✨ Upgrade banner on the Overview tab, or go to ⚙️ Settings and tap the Pro section. You'll be taken to a secure Stripe checkout to enter your card details. Once payment is complete, enter the code from your confirmation email to activate Pro.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
›
Open billing.stripe.com, log in with the email you used to subscribe, and cancel from there. You keep Pro until the end of your billing period.
💾Data & Backup
Can I use this on multiple devices?
›
Use ⚙️ Settings → Export Backup to download your data as a JSON file, then Import Backup on your other device to restore everything.
How do I export my data?
›
⚙️ Settings → Your Data → 💾 Export Backup. A file named wealthr-backup-[date].json downloads instantly containing all your data. Pro users can also export portfolio holdings as CSV.
Where does the backup save to?
›
📱 iPhone — Files app → Downloads folder. You may see a share sheet asking where to save it. 🤖 Android — Downloads folder, you will get a notification when done.
How do I restore from a backup?
›
⚙️ Settings → Your Data → 📂 Import Backup. Select your .json file. The app restores everything and reloads automatically.
How often should I back up?
›
At least once a week, especially after adding accounts or transactions. If your browser data is cleared, your app data goes with it.
How do I export a PDF report?
›
Go to ⚙️ Settings → Your Data → Export PDF. A summary report opens in a new tab — use your browser or device print function to save it as a PDF. The report includes your net worth, account balances, portfolio, loans and budget summary.
📊Net Worth
What is Total Net Worth?
›
Everything you own minus everything you owe — portfolio, bank, crypto, pension, property equity, minus credit cards and loans.
What is Liquid Net Worth?
›
Same as Total but excludes property equity and pensions since those cannot be accessed quickly. A more conservative measure of your actual spending power.
How does the history chart work?
›
Tap 📸 Save Today on the Overview tab to save a snapshot of your current net worth. Do this monthly to build a history chart over time. Up to 24 snapshots stored. This is a Pro feature.
How is property equity calculated?
›
Property equity = current property value minus outstanding mortgage. Only the equity (what you actually own) counts towards your net worth — not the full property value. Update both figures periodically using your mortgage statement and a property valuation tool like Zoopla or Rightmove.
How is the pension projection calculated?
›
The projection uses a compound growth formula based on your current pot value, monthly contributions (yours and employer), annual growth rate and years to retirement. The real value adjusts for 2% annual inflation. Projected monthly income uses the 4% safe withdrawal rule. It is an estimate — actual returns will vary.
💵Budget, Bills & Income
What are the Budget, Bills, Income and Goals tabs?
›
The Budget tab has three sections: Spending (categories and monthly limits), Income (salary, rental and other regular income sources), and Bills & Subs (recurring payments, subscriptions, due dates and reminders). Switch between them using the pill tabs at the top. Savings Goals live on the Overview tab.
How do bill reminders work?
›
Add a bill in the Bills tab with a name, amount, cycle and due date. The app checks for upcoming bills on open and sends a notification if one is due soon. Works best when installed as a PWA or via Google Play.
What billing cycles are supported?
›
Monthly (same date), every 4 weeks (28 days rolling), weekly, quarterly and annually. The app automatically tracks the next due date for each cycle.
How do I track subscriptions?
›
Go to Budget → tap 🔔 Bills & Subs, then use the 📺 Subs filter to view just subscriptions. Tap + Sub to add one — set name, amount, billing cycle and an optional start/end date. The monthly cost is included in the Budget summary and the Income vs Spending chart. Set an end date to cancel a subscription without losing its history.
How do I use Savings Goals?
›
Go to the Overview tab and open the 🎯 Savings Goals section. Add a goal with a name, target amount, how much you have saved so far, and your monthly contribution. The app calculates how many months until you reach your target and shows a progress ring. Tap any ring to quickly update how much you've saved. Tap Edit ✎ on a goal card to update the saved amount in detail.
What are start and end dates on bills, subscriptions and income?
›
Start dates tell the chart when a bill, subscription or income source began — so it only counts for months after that date. End dates mark when something stopped — it disappears from your active lists but stays in the chart for the months it was active. This keeps your history accurate without deleting old data.
📈Charts & Analytics
How does the Income vs Spending chart work?
›
The chart shows up to 12 months of data where you have income or spending recorded. Each month shows two bars side by side — green for total income and red for total spending. The surplus line shows whether you came out ahead or behind each month — toggle it on or off using the button above the chart.
How does loan payoff tracking work?
›
Add a loan with the amount owed, interest rate and monthly payment. The app simulates month-by-month amortisation to calculate your exact payoff date and total interest remaining. Monthly repayments are included in the Income vs Spending chart as an outgoing.
📈Stocks & Crypto
How do live stock prices work?
›
Stock prices are fetched automatically via Yahoo Finance. US, UK and European tickers are all supported. Tap ↻ Refresh on the Portfolio or Overview tab to update prices.
How do I add UK stocks?
›
Add tickers with the .LON suffix — e.g. BARC.LON, VOD.LON, LLOY.LON — and they will fetch automatically alongside your US stocks.
Why are stock prices slightly off?
›
Each position is shown in its own native currency (e.g. AAPL in USD, BARC.LON in GBP, ASML.AS in EUR). Prices may have a short delay vs other apps because they are fetched on demand rather than streaming. Net worth converts everything to your base currency using live exchange rates, so small differences from other tools are normal.
Can I track commodities?
›
Yes — add commodities in the Portfolio tab using futures tickers: GC=F (Gold), SI=F (Silver), CL=F (Crude Oil), BZ=F (Brent Crude), NG=F (Natural Gas), HG=F (Copper), PL=F (Platinum). Prices update automatically when you tap ↻ Refresh.
How do crypto prices work?
›
Crypto prices are fetched via CoinGecko and converted to your base currency. Tap ↻ Refresh on the Crypto tab or Overview to update prices.
My coin is not showing a price
›
Check you are using the correct symbol (BTC, ETH, SOL etc). Less common coins may not be supported. Verify the symbol on CoinGecko.
How do I change my currency?
›
⚙️ Settings → Base Currency. Choose from GBP (£), USD ($) or EUR (€). All values convert automatically using live exchange rates, and bank lists, pension providers and tax brackets update to match your region.
🔧Technical
What third party services does Wealthr use?
›
Yahoo Finance (stock prices), CoinGecko (crypto prices & gold), TradingView (interactive charts), Stripe (Pro payments), open.er-api.com (exchange rates), BBC / Guardian / FT / Sky / CNBC / Yahoo Finance / Bloomberg / Investing.com (news feeds), and Land Registry (UK sold prices — UK users only). These are only contacted when you actively use those features. Central bank rates (BoE / Fed / ECB) are shown from a built-in value — no API call.
